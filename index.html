The idea

We are not a fund.

A fund invests money it raised from others, on a fixed mandate and timeline, so it answers to those investors and there is pressure to make each company fit the model. We invest our own capital, one deal at a time, so backing you is genuine conviction rather than a quota to fill, and many of our circle are operators who bring introductions, judgement, and access to new markets, with real depth across the world.

Focus

Where we are looking.

Longevity

The science and business of a longer, healthier life, from diagnostics and clinics to consumer health.

Artificial intelligence

Applied AI, where real products are changing how industries work rather than promising to.

Luxury goods

Premium brands with durable appeal and the room to grow into new markets.

These are the areas drawing our attention today, but we are not a thesis-bound fund. Conviction can come from any sector, and the companies we back most often arrive from somewhere we were not looking. If you are building something exceptional, we want to see it, whatever the category.
